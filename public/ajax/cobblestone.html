Cobblestone and Stilettos

After a stint working in SEO, I would scour the streets of Old City, Philadelphia on my lunch break for vintage shops and hidden gems. Combining my love for writing, fashion and graphics by curating the City of Brotherly Love's shopping scene on Cobblestone & Stilettos. The Wordpress blog was a curation of events, brand and boutique information, with a "sketch-book"-style collage graphics designed Photoshop and Illustrator, highlighting each store's aesthetic & vibe.
