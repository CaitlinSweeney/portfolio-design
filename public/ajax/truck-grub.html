Truck Grub Hub

As a full-stack web development student at RefactorU, we had one week to build a web application, using MEAN stack frameworks. After witnessing the popularity of the food truck culture in the U.S., a real-time, Food Truck Finder is in order. Still in its infancy, Truck Grub Hub provides a login page for food truck owners to store their data in a MongoDB and share their current geolocation on Google maps. Version 2 will include sorted truck information for visitors to find their "craving" and a place for breweries to update the food trucks near their location.
